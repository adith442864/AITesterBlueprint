# ShopEasy Refund & Return Policy

## Return Windows
- Standard items can be returned within 30 days of purchase.
- Electronics have a 15-day return window.
- Sale items are final sale and cannot be returned.
- Gift cards and downloadable software are non-refundable.

## Return Conditions
- Items must be in original packaging and unused condition.
- A receipt or order confirmation is required for all returns.
- Items damaged by the customer are not eligible for return.

## Refund Processing
- Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after receiving the returned item.
- Refunds are issued to the original payment method.
- Store credit is available as an alternative and is processed instantly.

## Shipping
- Free return shipping is available for orders over $50.
- Orders under $50 require the customer to pay return shipping.
- International returns are the responsibility of the customer.

## Exchanges
- Exchanges are available for size or color changes within 30 days.
- Electronics exchanges follow the 15-day window.
- Exchange shipping is always free.
