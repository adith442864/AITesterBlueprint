# ShopEasy Account & Subscription Help

## Account Management
- Create an account at shopEasy.com/signup with email or Google SSO.
- Password must be at least 8 characters with one number and one special character.
- Two-factor authentication (2FA) is available and recommended.
- Account deletion requests are processed within 30 days.

## Subscription Plans
- Free tier: basic browsing, wishlist, order tracking.
- ShopEasy Plus ($9.99/month): free shipping on all orders, early access to sales, priority support.
- ShopEasy Premium ($19.99/month): all Plus features + same-day delivery in metro areas + exclusive deals.

## Cancellation
- Cancel subscriptions anytime via Account > Billing > Cancel Subscription.
- Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing cycle.
- No refunds for partial billing periods.
- Cancelled accounts retain free-tier access.

## Payment Methods
- Accepted: Visa, Mastercard, Amex, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay.
- Payment information is encrypted and stored securely.
- Failed payments are retried twice over 48 hours before subscription suspension.

## Support
- Live chat: available 9 AM - 9 PM EST, Monday through Saturday.
- Email support: support@shopEasy.com, response within 24 hours.
- Phone support: 1-800-SHOP-EASY, available for Plus and Premium subscribers.
- Priority support queue for Premium subscribers with < 5 minute wait time.
