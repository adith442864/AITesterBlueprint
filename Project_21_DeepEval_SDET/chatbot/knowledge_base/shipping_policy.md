# ShopEasy Shipping Policy

## Domestic Shipping
- Standard shipping: 5-7 business days, free on orders over $35.
- Express shipping: 2-3 business days, $9.99 flat rate.
- Next-day shipping: available in metro areas only, $19.99.

## International Shipping
- Available to 45 countries.
- Delivery takes 10-21 business days.
- Customs duties and taxes are the responsibility of the customer.
- International orders over $100 get free standard international shipping.

## Order Tracking
- Tracking numbers are emailed within 24 hours of shipment.
- Track orders at shopEasy.com/track or via the mobile app.
- SMS tracking updates are available by texting TRACK to 55123.

## Delivery Issues
- If a package is marked delivered but not received, contact support within 48 hours.
- Lost packages are investigated within 5 business days.
- Damaged packages must be reported within 72 hours with photos.

## Holiday Shipping
- Holiday cut-off dates are posted each November.
- Gift wrapping is available for $4.99 per item.
- Holiday returns have an extended window through January 31st.
